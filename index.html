About Playbook Interactive

HR that feels human, not transactional.

A modern, people-first HR consultancy with a behavioural-psychology edge. We guide teams, shape culture, and create workplaces where people can thrive.

At Playbook Interactive, we're a startup on a mission to make HR human, collaborative, and engaging. We partner with organisations to design workplaces where people feel valued, leaders feel empowered, and teams work better together. Think of us as your HR coaches — offering guidance, strategies, and insights that help you craft your own approach.

HR should be intentional, thoughtful, and designed for the people who make your organisation thrive.
Our founder story

Built on the belief that HR should feel human.

Playbook Interactive was created with a simple belief: HR should feel human, not transactional. After seeing how often organisations struggle with rigid, one-size-fits-all approaches, we set out to build something different — an approach that is practical, collaborative, and tailored to each team.

As a growing startup, we bring fresh perspective, flexibility, and a hands-on mindset to every partnership. Our goal is to help organisations move away from "checkbox HR" and toward meaningful, people-first practices that truly support their teams.
